==========================
Relay Pin Assignment
==========================

+----------------------------+--------------------------+------------------------------------+
| Wire Color                 | Assigned Relay Port      | Signal/Label                       |
+============================+==========================+====================================+
| Yellow                     | NO                       | Normally Open                      |
+----------------------------+--------------------------+------------------------------------+
| Green                      | NC                       | Normally Closed                    |
+----------------------------+--------------------------+------------------------------------+
| Red or Orange              | COM                      | Common                             |
+----------------------------+--------------------------+------------------------------------+

+---------------------------+-----------------------------------------+

+---------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| Gray                      | Channel 2 Common                        |
+---------------------------+-----------------------------------------+

+---------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
